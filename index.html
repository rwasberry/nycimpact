Who We Are

More Than
A Club.

Founded in 2011, NYC Impact gives young athletes in New York City a real shot at competitive volleyball — regardless of background. We train hard, compete harder, and build the kind of confidence that follows you off the court.

01
Compete

AAU tournaments, NACIVT, regional and national competition across all age groups.

02
Develop

Position-specific skills clinics, private lessons, and structured season training.

03
Belong

A community built in New York City — diverse, tight-knit, and rooted in respect for the game.
