Why It Matters

More Kids
On The Court.

NYC Impact focuses on the personal and athletic development of young athletes across the city — building a safe, positive environment where student-athletes chase individual excellence inside a team setting.

Membership fees only cover part of what it takes to run six programs at a competitive level. Donations close that gap — funding the equipment, travel, and scholarships that keep the door open for families who couldn't otherwise afford to play.

Access

Full and half scholarships so membership cost never decides who gets to play.

Equipment

Balls, carts, net systems, and medical supplies that keep practices running safely.

Competition

Tournament entry fees and travel costs for local, regional, and national play.
